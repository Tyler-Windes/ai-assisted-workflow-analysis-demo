#### Stakeholder Summary

The sample workflow review identified recurring data-quality issues that would reduce reporting reliability and make prioritization less consistent.

##### Key Findings

- Non-standard priority labels reduce SLA and escalation visibility.
- Missing assigned teams delay ownership and make active work harder to manage.
- Closed records without resolution notes weaken traceability.
- Duplicate indicators suggest recurring intake and categorization problems.
- Inconsistent system-area naming makes trend analysis less reliable.

##### Recommended Next Steps

1. Standardize required intake fields.
2. Normalize priority and status values.
3. Require ownership before work begins.
4. Require resolution notes before closure.
5. Review duplicate indicators weekly.
6. Run data-quality checks before KPI reporting.
7. Use AI-assisted classification only as draft support with human review.
